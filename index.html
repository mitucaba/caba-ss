Latest Stories
Supporting Our Hardworking Farmers!

Fuel subsidies were provided to farmers across all barangays in Caba, helping ease the impact of rising fuel costs amid the Middle East crisis. Headed by Mayor Genefer Garcia Bulao Madriaga, this initiative reflects our continued commitment to assist our farmers in these challengiing times.

Caba’s Intricately Crafted Float Stuns Crowd

Caba’s award-winning float captivated audiences with its intricate details—from hand-crafted ornaments and layered textures to vibrant patterns that beautifully reflected local culture, artistry, and meticulous craftsmanship.

One Step Toward A Debt Free Caba

Mayor Madriaga has officially settled the town's P15,938,710.77 financial obligation and loan balance on its Php 25M loan under Promissory Note No. 2024-046(June 13, 2024). Upon taking her oath, the mayor made a firm commitment to lead Caba toward a financial freedom - and true to her word, we see the start of that promise fulfilled.

BRP Diego Silang (FFG-07): Philippine Navy’s Newest Guided Missile Frigate

Held at Naval Operating Base Subic in December 2025, the commissioning of BRP Diego Silang (FFG-07) gathered key officials and distinguished guests—including Mayor Madriaga—to witness the Philippine Navy’s newest guided missile frigate officially enter service.
